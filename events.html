Featured Events

2024 Winners and Events

Miss Gay Queen Cainta 2025

A pageant celebrating the talents and beauty of Cainta's LGBTQ+ community, featuring 14 contestants showcasing vibrant SumBingTik-themed costumes.

Caindakan Sa Kalsada 2025

A lively street dance competition where participants from various schools don colorful costumes and perform energetic dances to rhythmic drumbeats and native gongs.

Sereneta 2025

An evening of orchestral music where different bands, including the Cainta Community Band, serenade attendees, adding a classical touch to the festival.

Color Run 2025

A fun-filled, untimed 3 to 5-kilometer run where participants are joyfully doused with colored powders at each kilometer, promoting health and community engagement. ​

Sumbingtik House Decoration Opening 2025

A vibrant display where houses in Barangay Sto. Niño are adorned with local delicacies like suman, bibingka, and latik, and decorated with large SumBingTik-themed props..

Youth Can Dance 2025

A hip-hop dance crew competition showcasing the dynamic dance talents of Cainta's youth, fostering creativity and community pride.

One Cainta Biker's Fun Ride 2025

A community cycling event promoting health and wellness, where participants enjoy a leisurely ride through the streets of Cainta.

Interown Recycled Christmas Tree Lighting 2025

An eco-friendly event where recycled materials are used to create illuminated Christmas trees, enhancing the festive spirit and promoting environmental awareness.
